COVID-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus.
The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flue) with symtomps such as a cough, fever and in more severe cases, difficulty breathing. You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently, avoiding touching your face and avoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell.


How it spreads?

Coronavirus disease spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a subject or object that has the virus on it, then touches their eyes, nose or mouth.
